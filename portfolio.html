Vocab Trainer Application

An application to find Spanish words and their definitions along with example sentences. These items can later be added to a vocab list to have a list of words with definitions and example sentences. The use of definitions and examples in the targeted language is ideal for intermediate language learners that have a sufficient understanding of the language and want to improve their vocabulary and comprehension in a natural way.
